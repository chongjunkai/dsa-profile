Interview notes

What I hope to learn next.

I am looking for a school environment where I can deepen my computing knowledge, collaborate with motivated peers, and contribute through curiosity, effort, and teamwork.

Why are you applying through DSA?

Computing is more than a subject I enjoy. It is how I explore ideas and challenge myself. DSA would let me learn in a community that shares that interest and contribute the experience I have gained from coding, robotics, and team competitions.

What are you most proud of?

I am proud not only of awards, but of becoming more persistent. Competitions and projects taught me that progress often comes after a failed attempt, careful debugging, and the willingness to ask for feedback.

How will you contribute to the school?

I hope to contribute ideas to computing or robotics projects, support teammates, and share what I learn. I also want to bring the discipline from piano and teamwork from football into school life.
